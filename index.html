Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

1. Who We Are

BillScan AI is a GST invoice extraction service built for Indian CA firms and businesses. Contact: support.billscan@gmail.com

2. What We Collect

  • Google account data — your name, email address, and profile picture, collected when you sign in via Google OAuth. Used solely to identify your account.
  • IP address — logged transiently to enforce the free-tier quota (2 extractions per IP). Not linked to personal identity and not shared.
  • Subscription and usage data — your plan type (free/starter/pro) and monthly extraction count, stored securely in our database.
  • Payment data — handled entirely by Razorpay. We never receive, see, or store your card or bank details.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your invoice files — every document is processed in RAM and immediately discarded. Nothing is written to disk or logged.
  • The content of your invoices (vendor names, amounts, GSTINs) — never stored, never transmitted beyond the AI extraction step.
  • Your invoices are never used to train any AI model.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and keep you signed in (30-day session token stored in your browser)
  • To track extraction quotas and enforce plan limits
  • To manage your subscription and process refund requests
  • To send transactional emails (subscription confirmation, renewal) via Razorpay

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party service providers for authentication, payment processing, AI extraction, and infrastructure hosting. These providers are contractually bound to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify. Your payment details are handled entirely by our payment processor — we never store card or bank information. Invoice content shared with AI providers for extraction is not retained or used for training purposes.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data (Google ID, email, plan): retained while your account is active
  • Anonymous IP usage data: automatically purged after 30 days
  • Invoice content: zero retention — never persisted

7. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, email support.billscan@gmail.com. We will respond within 72 hours.

8. Cookies

We use one session cookie during Google sign-in (cleared after authentication completes). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Contact

For privacy queries: support.billscan@gmail.com

🔒 Your invoice data is never stored. Zero retention is a core product guarantee, not just a policy.
