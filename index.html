Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 · Effective immediately

🔒 The short version: We do not store your invoices. We do not store extracted data. Your information is processed in memory and discarded the moment you get your results. We have nothing to sell, nothing to leak.

1. Who we are

BillScan AI is an AI-powered GST invoice data extraction service. We are a technology tool, not an accounting firm, tax advisor, or financial institution. We are registered in India and operate under Indian law.

2. What data we collect

When you use BillScan AI:

  • Invoice files — uploaded by you, processed in memory, never written to disk or database, discarded immediately after extraction.
  • Extracted data — returned to your browser only. Not stored on our servers.
  • API keys — entered by you, stored only in your own browser's local storage. Never transmitted to our servers.
  • Basic server logs — HTTP method, path, response code, response time. No request body, no file content, no extracted data is ever logged.

We do not collect: names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment card details, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us.

3. How we use your data

We use invoice files exclusively to perform the extraction you request. No other use. No training of AI models on your data. No analysis. No profiling.

4. Data storage and retention

Invoice files: 0 seconds retention. Processed in RAM, never written to storage, buffer wiped after response is sent.

Extracted results: 0 seconds retention. Returned to your browser and not stored anywhere on our infrastructure.

Server logs: 30 days. Contain no invoice content — only technical metadata (HTTP status codes, response times).

5. Payments

All payments are processed by Razorpay (Razorpay Software Private Limited). Your card number, UPI ID, bank details, and OTPs are entered directly on Razorpay's platform. BillScan AI receives only a payment success or failure notification and a transaction reference ID. We never see, store, or handle your payment credentials.

6. Third-party AI providers

Invoice processing uses AI models from third-party providers (Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, or others). Your invoice is sent to these providers solely to perform extraction. These providers have their own privacy policies. We use their APIs under terms that restrict training on customer data.

7. Your rights under DPDP Act 2023

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to access, correct, and erase personal data we hold about you. Since we do not store invoice data, there is typically nothing to access or erase. For server log inquiries, contact us at the address below.

8. Children

BillScan AI is a professional B2B tool intended for use by CA firms and business professionals. It is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: contact us via WhatsApp or email listed on this website. We respond within 2 business days.
